Privacy Policy

This privacy policy outlines how your personal information is collected, used, disclosed, and protected when you use the Pix Imagine application.

Information Collection and Usage

PixImagine collects and uses the following information through user-uploaded images and user interactions:

  • User-provided images: The application analyzes and works with the images you provide.
  • User interactions: Data related to your app usage is collected and used to improve our services and personalize the user experience.

Information Sharing and Disclosure

PixImagine does not share, sell, or rent personal information of users to third parties, except when required by legal obligations, applicable laws, or court orders.

Security

PixImagine employs industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information. However, complete security of data transmitted over the internet cannot be guaranteed.

Changes

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Notifications about changes may be made on our website or within our application.
